Who am I?

I am a Cognitive Engineering student attending the ENSC, the National School of Cognitics in France. We focus on human-technology interactions to create innovative solutions that enhance user experience and safety.

Passionate about the challenges raised by human factors applied to new technologies, I intend to pursue my career as a research engineer focused on innovation.

Projects

AI TOOL PROTOTYPE FOR BLENDER® SOFTWARE (2024)

Research problem exploration by prototyping an AI tool to help a 3D animator to materialize complex motion ideas. The tool should help the animator to create realistic movements or correct rigid or unrealistic movements.

Software: Figma®

AI FOR CREATIVITY AND PRODUCTIVITY (2024)

Creation of multimedia content using AI tools to boost our creativity and productivity. These tools are used for:
-3D character generation
-Character movement
-Character voice
-Character speech
-Automatic subtitles
-Background image and video editing

Software: Meshy.ai, Deep Motion, Replica Studios, ChatGPT, VEED.ai, Canva

RESEARCH INTERNSHIP AT INRIA (2024)

Development of a 3D Human Machine Interface with Unity that can be explored using face and eye movements. In the longer term, the project will explore the impact of exploration on the perception of 3D scene features. E.g.: assessment of change blindness.

Software: Unity®, C#, Computer Vision, Eyetracking, Headtracking

CAPTAIN OF ENSC'S CHEER TEAM (2024)

Leadership of ENSC's cheer club, involving creation of choreographies and soundtracks, music editing, and management of the club's finances and social media presence. We designed a theatrical choreography inspired by the theme of 'LOVE' and earned 50% of the jury's 'favorite show' awards.

Software: Audacity, Canva, Online Sequencer (8-bit music generation)

INDIVIDUAL COMPUTER PROJECT (2024)

Development of a tool that enables pianists to play pieces composed of several pages without interruption, by detecting in real time the notes played and automatically turning the pages when necessary.

Software: Python, Fourier Transform

JUST POMPOM (2023)

Elaboration of a motion-based dance video game embedded in a web platform. The aim of this project is to help players perfect their dancing and reach the highest level. It uses Mediapipe® to compare the player's dance movements with those of a pom dance captain and estimate the precision of their movements.

Software: Mediapipe®, Figma, Html, CSS

VIRTUAL COMPANION (2023)

3D design, modelling and animation of a virtual character for the emotional support of post-stroke patients. Implementation in a "seven differences" game as helper to test its efficiency.

Software: Blender®, Unity®

INSTANT SIGN LANGUAGE TRANSLATION (2022)

Development of a python program using artificial intelligence to instantly translate French sign language, starting with numbers. Use of the following algorithmes: K Nearest Neighbor (KNN), Artificial and Convolutional Neural Network (A-CNN).

Software: Python, Mediapipe®, Tensorflow®, Blender®

WIRELESS DYNAMIC CHARGING (2021)

Faisability study of a wireless charging road for electric vehicules. The purpose was to confirm or refute the potential of this project. As batteries are the biggest challenge for electric vehicules, the use of induction may enhance their popularity.

Tools: Induction, capacitor
